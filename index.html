Crystal Collector

Instructions

You'll be shown a random number at the start of the game along with four crystals.
Each one, when clicked will add a certain amount of points to your 'total score'.
You will not know the value of the crystal until clicked on.
You win by determining the value of each crystal and clicking them so that your 'total score' is equal to the random number displayed at the start of each game.
If your guesses puts you over the random number, you lose (think Blackjack).
Once you've won or lost the value of the crystals is randomized and a new, random number to guess is shown.
